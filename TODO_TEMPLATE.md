## To‑Do List Templates

Use the sections below as copy/paste templates. Replace bracketed placeholders. Check off boxes as you complete items.

---

### Quick List (Lightweight)

- [ ] Task: <short title>
  - Priority: P1 | P2 | P3
  - Owner: <name>
  - Due: <YYYY-MM-DD>
  - Status: Todo | In Progress | Blocked | Done
  - Notes: <context/next step>

Repeat the above block for each task, or use the quick table:

| [ ] | Task | Priority | Owner | Due | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| [ ] | <title> | P1/P2/P3 | <name> | <date> | Todo/In Progress/Blocked/Done | <notes> |

---

### Project/Sprint Template (Detailed)

#### Overview
- Goal: <clear outcome>
- Scope: <what’s in/out>
- Timeframe: <start → end>
- Success Criteria: <measurable>

#### Now / Next / Later
- Now: <top 3 items>
- Next: <queued items>
- Later: <parking lot>

#### Task Card
##### <Task Title>
- ID: <unique id>
- Type: Feature | Bug | Chore | Research
- Priority: P1 | P2 | P3
- Status: Todo | In Progress | Blocked | Review | Done
- Owner: <name>
- Due: <YYYY-MM-DD>
- Estimate: <e.g., 3h or 2 pts>
- Dependencies: <links/ids>
- Definition of Done:
  - [ ] Criteria 1
  - [ ] Criteria 2
- Subtasks:
  - [ ] Step 1
  - [ ] Step 2
- Risks/Blockers: <what could slip>
- Notes/Context: <links, decisions>

#### Daily Check‑In
- Yesterday: <what moved>
- Today: <next actions>
- Blockers: <help needed>

#### Review
- Outcomes: <what shipped>
- Lessons: <keep/change>
- Follow‑ups: <carry‑over tasks>

---

### Minimal One‑Pager

**Top 5 Priorities**
1. [ ] <task>
2. [ ] <task>
3. [ ] <task>
4. [ ] <task>
5. [ ] <task>

**Key Dates**: <milestones>

**Notes**: <bullets>

---

Tips
- Keep tasks actionable (verb‑led) and ≤ 14 words.
- Prefer "Now/Next/Later" for focus; archive the rest.
- Update status daily; close the loop with a short Review.
